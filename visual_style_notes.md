# Star Raiders Visual Style Notes

## Box Art Observations (Model CXL4011)
- Original Atari 400/800 Computer Adventure game
- Published by Atari, A Warner Communications Company

## Key Visual Elements
- Player ship: Angular, geometric design with blue/white coloring
- Enemy ships: Include distinctive yellow/gold pyramid-shaped vessels (likely Zylon ships)
- Space environment: Dark starfield with colorful nebula effects (pink/purple)
- Grid-based space representation: Visible grid pattern extending into distance
- Color palette: High contrast with bright ships against dark space, vibrant energy effects
- Art style: 1970s sci-fi aesthetic with vector-like geometric shapes

## Design Philosophy
- Emphasis on geometric, angular spacecraft designs
- Clear visual distinction between player and enemy vessels
- Grid system suggests tactical/strategic gameplay element
- Dramatic space combat atmosphere with energy weapons visible
