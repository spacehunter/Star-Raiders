# Attack Computer Display

## Overview
- Appears at bottom right corner of screen during front view of space
- Target marker in middle used to track and locate enemy targets or friendly starbases
- Centering the target marker shows indicator marks on edges of display

## Lock-On Indicators
- **A marks**: Computer has horizontally locked photons onto target
- **B marks**: Computer has horizontally AND vertically locked photons onto target  
- **C marks**: Target is locked horizontally, vertically, AND within range - BEST time to fire
  - 90% kill probability guaranteed when C appears

## Special Notes
- Using computer lock-on is the best procedure for destroying a Zylon BASESTAR
- When θ = +0 and Φ = +0, target is centered
- In empty sectors, Attack Computer Display will be empty
- When all enemy targets destroyed in a sector, target marker disappears from display

## Visual Layout
The display shows a rectangular frame with:
- Two A indicators on top left and right corners
- Two B indicators on middle left and right sides  
- Two C indicators at bottom center (left and right of center)
- Target crosshair in the center of display
