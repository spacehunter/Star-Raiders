# Sub-Space Radio

## Overview
The Sub-Space Radio is used for updating the Galactic Chart with critical mission information.

## Functions
- Relays messages: "STARBASE SURROUNDED" and "STARBASE DESTROYED"
- Generates the targets indicator displayed on the Galactic Chart

## Damage Behavior
- If Sub-Space Radio is damaged, Galactic Chart will not be updated
- No radio communication functions can be performed when damaged
- Moving cursor around Galactic Chart will still indicate number of enemy targets in each sector on the TARGETS indicator at bottom of screen (even when damaged)
