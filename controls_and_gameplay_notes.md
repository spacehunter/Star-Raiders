# Star Raiders - Controls and Gameplay

## Difficulty Level Visuals
The game shows four difficulty levels with distinct visual representations:
- **NOVICE**: Basic starfield view
- **PILOT**: Enhanced visuals with energy beams
- **WARRIOR**: More complex combat scenarios
- **COMMANDER**: Most challenging with full tactical elements

## Joystick Controls

### Front View Controls
- **Fire Button**: Fire photon torpedoes
- **Forward to Dive**: Push stick forward
- **RIGHT/Starboard**: Push stick right
- **LIFT**: Pull stick back
- **LEFT/Port**: Push stick left
- **CLIMBS**: Pull stick back
- **DIVE**: Push stick forward
- **Back to Climb**: Pull stick back

### Aft View Controls
- **Fire Button**: Fire photon torpedoes
- **Forward Climb**: Push stick forward
- **RIGHT/Turns Ship left**: Push stick right
- **LEFT**: Push stick left
- **DIVE**: Pull stick back
- **Back to Dive**: Pull stick back

### Control Stick Operation
- Vertical movement: Push forward to dive, pull back to climb
- Horizontal movement: Push left/right to turn ship
- The stick controls ship orientation in 3D space
- Front and aft views have different control mappings

## Keyboard Controls

### Main Control Keys (0-9)
- **0-9**: Twin-Ion Engine Control (velocity/speed)
- Also aborts hyperwarp

### Letter Keys
- **C**: Computer Attack Control
- **F**: Front View
- **G**: Galactic Chart
- **H**: Hyperwarp, Engines Engaged
- **L**: Long Range Sector Scan
- **M**: Manual Target Selector
- **S**: Shields
- **T**: Tracking Computer
- **P**: Pause (suspends game)

### Twin-Ion Engine Control (0-9)
- Twin-Ion engines maneuver starship within sector
- Keys select velocity or speed of starship
- Cartridge displays speed at far left of control panel display

## Console Controls
- **POWER ON/OFF switch**: Must be ON
- **Control Stick**: Firmly plugged in
- **Cartridge**: Firmly in place
- **SELECT button**: Choose mission difficulty level (cycles through NOVICE, PILOT, WARRIOR, COMMANDER)
- **START button**: Begin mission after selecting difficulty

## Display Views

### F - Front View
Shows view of space in front of starship

### A - Aft View  
Shows view of space behind starship

### Attack Computer Display
- Displays crosshair and lock-on indicators
- Shows Attack Computer Display at lower right corner of screen
- Computer updates horizontal (⊖), vertical (⊕), and range (R) coordinates of attacking Zylon starship
- Locks photon torpedoes onto target line (section explains lock-on indicators A, B, C)

### G - Galactic Chart
Most important tool during each mission. Provides display of entire galaxy divided into sectors on screen. Each sector contains:
- Enemy starships
- Friendly starbase
- Or is uninhabited

Chart uses symbols:
- Sector with < means one enemy target
- Sector with = means two enemy starships
- Sector with > means three enemy starships (also known as task-force)
- Sector with ≥ means four enemy starships (fleet)
- Sector with * indicates friendly starbase

When sector has ≥ and *, enemy starships are in process of surrounding and destroying starbase in that sector.

Two dots on Galactic Chart:
- One dot represents present position
- Other dot (moves via Control Stick) indicates hyperwarp destination

## Mission Structure

### Starting Mission
1. Press SELECT to choose difficulty level
2. Each press cycles through difficulty options
3. Display shows mission level at top of screen
4. Press START to begin
5. Keyboard displays "STAR RAIDERS" to NOVICE monitor users
6. Mission begins, rating displayed after based on score

### Mission Objectives
- Destroy all enemy starships without allowing them to destroy starbases
- Complete mission in as little time as possible with minimal energy expended
- Prevent friendly starbases from being destroyed

### Energy Management
- Warp Engines Engaged: Important for travel and hyperwarp
- Must move dot on Galactic Chart to sector destination
- Travel via hyperwarp, then "hyperspace"
- Warp Engines Engaged frequently to mark mission progress and track enemy starship movements
- Enemy starships shift periodically in attempt to surround and destroy starbases

## Key Gameplay Notes
- Control Stick right/left in aft view has opposite effect from front view
- Steering with Control Stick takes practice
- The OPTION button has no effect in Star Raiders
- Control Panel Display shows various readouts (IV represents specific display elements)
